Why Choose Time Reporter?

Time Reporter is designed to simplify your Toastmasters meetings by providing professional timing tools, automated reporting, and seamless rule customization. Whether you’re a Timekeeper, VPE, or Speaker — this app helps you stay on track and improve meeting efficiency.

Key Features

Smart Timer

Visual color cues (green/yellow/red) with vibration and sound alerts. Built-in Toastmasters rules and custom timing available.

Meeting Records

Automatically save speech times, meeting dates, and rule types. Easy browsing of past sessions in chronological order.

Visual Reports

Generate charts and PDF reports instantly. Share results with members via email, chat, or cloud storage.

Custom Rules

Create personalized timing rules, configure thresholds, grace periods, and reminders. Sync across devices with iCloud.
